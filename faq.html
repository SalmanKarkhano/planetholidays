Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your perfect holiday with Planet Holidays.

Booking & Payments

01 How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance for the best availability and rates, especially during peak season (December–April). For popular resorts or honeymoon packages, 4–6 months is ideal. We can also accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.
02 What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers, major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and digital payment platforms. A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due 30 days before departure. Full payment terms will be outlined in your booking confirmation.
03 Can I customize my package? +
Absolutely — every trip we plan is fully customizable. Choose your preferred resort, room type, meal plan, activities, and special add-ons. We build the package around your preferences and budget. Just tell us what you're dreaming of and we'll make it happen.
04 What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations 90+ days before departure receive a full deposit refund less a $50 admin fee. 60–89 days: 50% of booking value retained. 30–59 days: 75% retained. Less than 30 days: no refund. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance. See our Terms of Service for full details.

Travel Practicalities

05 Do I need a visa for the Maldives? +
The Maldives offers a free 30-day tourist visa on arrival for virtually all nationalities — no prior application needed. You'll need a valid passport (at least 6 months validity), proof of onward travel, confirmed accommodation, and sufficient funds. For Sri Lanka, most passport holders require an Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) obtained online before arrival.
06 Are airport transfers included? +
Yes! We arrange all transfers — speedboat, seaplane, or domestic flights — depending on your resort's location. Resorts in North and South Malé Atolls typically use speedboats (20–45 min). More distant resorts require a domestic flight plus speedboat. Everything is coordinated seamlessly so you don't have to worry about logistics.
07 What is the best time to visit the Maldives? +
The Maldives has two main seasons. The dry season (November–April) offers the best weather — clear skies, calm seas, and excellent visibility for diving. Peak season is December–April. The wet season (May–October) brings more rain and rougher seas, but also better deals and fewer crowds. The islands are beautiful year-round and most resorts operate regardless of season.
08 Is travel insurance mandatory? +
Travel insurance is not legally mandatory, but we strongly recommend it. Given the remote nature of many Maldivian resorts, medical evacuation costs can be extremely high. Look for a policy covering medical emergencies, evacuation, trip cancellation, and lost baggage. We can recommend suitable insurers on request.

Resorts & Packages

09 What's included in your packages? +
Our packages typically include accommodation, your chosen meal plan (bed & breakfast, half-board, full-board, or all-inclusive), airport transfers, and 24/7 concierge support. Activities, excursions, spa treatments, and international flights can be added based on your preferences. We provide a detailed inclusions list with every quote.
10 Which resort is right for me? +
The right resort depends on your priorities. Bandos is great for families and snorkeling. Olhuveli offers overwater villas for romantic escapes. Hudhuran Fushi is ideal for surfers. Kandooma suits adventure-seekers. Malahini Kuda Bandos is our most budget-friendly option. Tell us your travel style and we'll find the perfect match.
11 Can you arrange honeymoon surprises? +
Absolutely! We specialise in creating magical honeymoon experiences. From private beach dinners and rose petal turndowns to sunset cruises and couples' spa treatments — we coordinate all the special touches with the resort. Just let us know what you'd like and we'll take care of everything discreetly.
12 Do you offer group travel and corporate events? +
Yes — we have dedicated MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) services for groups and corporate clients. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, incentive trip, conference, or team-building event, we can handle everything from venue sourcing to logistics. Visit our Events page for more details or contact us directly.
