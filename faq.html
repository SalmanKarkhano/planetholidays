We recommend booking at least 3–6 months ahead, especially for high season (November–April). Popular overwater villas at partner resorts fill up quickly. For honeymoon or special-occasion trips, even earlier is ideal so we can secure the best options for you.
Most nationalities receive a free 30-day visa on arrival in the Maldives. You'll need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), a confirmed hotel booking, and proof of onward travel. For Sri Lanka, visa requirements vary by nationality — we'll handle all the details for your specific situation.

The dry season (November–April) offers the most predictable sunshine and calm seas, making it ideal for diving and beach days.
