FAQ

Common Questions

01 How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance for the best availability and rates, especially during peak season (December–April). However, we can accommodate last-minute requests depending on resort availability.
02 Do you handle visa arrangements? +
The Maldives offers a free 30-day tourist visa on arrival for most nationalities. We provide complete guidance on entry requirements, documentation, and any specific visa needs for your nationality.
03 Are airport transfers included? +
Yes! We arrange all transfers — speedboat, seaplane, or domestic flights — depending on your resort's location. Everything is coordinated seamlessly so you don't have to worry about logistics.
04 Can I customize my package? +
Absolutely. Every trip we plan is fully customizable. Choose your preferred resort, room type, meal plan, activities, and special add-ons. We build the package around your preferences and budget.
05 What's included in your packages? +
Our packages typically include accommodation, meal plans (based on your choice), airport transfers, and 24/7 concierge support. Activities, excursions, and flights can be added based on your preferences.
