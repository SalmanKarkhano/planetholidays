Who We Are

Born from Wanderlust,
Built on Trust

At Planet Holidays, we don't just plan trips — we craft experiences. Founded by passionate travelers in 2024, we are the travel division of Advance Engineering Consultant Pvt Ltd, a trusted entity since 2014.

Our expertise lies in inbound tourism, focusing on the enchanting Maldivian Islands and the rich cultural landscapes of Sri Lanka. Born from the desire to create exceptional holiday experiences in the breathtaking Maldivian Islands, our vision has led us to specialize in crafting moments that become lifelong memories.

We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional quality and personalized service to ensure every journey is smooth, joyful, and unforgettable. Leveraging our extensive industry connections, we guarantee unmatched value and curated travel solutions.
